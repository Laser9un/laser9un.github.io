Each coin SABR, FBOL coin and SFTZ is fully backed 1/500 to the ounce of Gold, through our association with Gold supplier. Gold the money standard for thousands of years is merged with block chain technology to create real world value with block chain speed and efficiency, this is what SABR coin brings to the world.

ALTA SABR FUND

SABR’s partnering with ALTA yields unique synergies leveraging ALTA’s demonstrated worldwide financial experiences and successes in creative investment structures, meeting and fulfilling complex investment objectives for both its domestic and international clients. Paul Kang, co-founder of ALTA/KPH, will serve as Chair and CIO of the Fund. Christoph Knedel, founder of KPH, will serve on the investment committee. Claire Yixin Zhang will lead investments as Co-CIO of the Fund.

SABR 25 by 2025

SABR ambition is to have 25 Nation States take advantage of SABR by creating a bespoke national block chain currency with all of the SABR attributes. Each Nation will have its fully enabled digital block chain currency leveling the playing field and allowing fully transparent, low cost, safe enabled transactions. This is what the block chain was created for and its intend on currencies, to bank the underbanked, to bank the unbankable.
